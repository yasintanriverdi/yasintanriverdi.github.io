GoalFlow Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 28, 2026

GoalFlow helps users create, organize, and manage goals on their device.

Information We Collect

GoalFlow stores goal content locally on your device.

The app may use third-party services such as Firebase and Google AdMob, which may collect limited technical and diagnostic information, analytics data, coarse location, device identifiers, advertising data, and app interaction data to support app functionality, analytics, and ad delivery.

We use information to operate the app, improve stability and performance, understand app usage, and display ads where applicable.

Your goals and descriptions are stored locally on your device unless you choose to remove the app or clear your device data.
